About Us

A Higher Education Revolution

Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a unique collaborative initiative between the governments of Pakistan and Austria. We follow the renowned "Fachhochschule" model, focusing on practical learning and industry-academia synergy.

Our mission is to transform Pakistan’s youth into a national dividend by providing world-class technical education in collaboration with global partners.

Why PAF-IAST?

Innovation Hub

Home to the Sino-Pak Center for Artificial Intelligence and cutting-edge Research Parks.

Global Partners

Collaborations with top Austrian, Chinese, and German institutes for technology transfer.

Skill-Based

Education designed to meet the demands of the modern global job market with practical internships.
